Availy alpha

Show your availability to colleagues

Especially when working in remote teams it can be hard to see where your colleagues actually are.
Availy solves this problem by providing slash commands for setting a status in Slack.
The status appears next to the name in form of an emoji 👌

Available commands

Check-in 🏠🏢

Start your day by checking in and tell where you work today

/check-in [home|office] [optional message]

Check-out 🏢🏠

End your day by checking out and say goodbye

/check-out [optional message]

Out for lunch 🍴

Tell your colleagues that you are out for lunch now

/ofl [optional message]

Back from lunch 🍴

End your lunch.

/bfl [optional message]

Out of office ➡️

You need to leave the office?

/out [optional message]

Back in office ➡️

Mark yourself as back in the office

/back [optional message]

Focus 👁

Let your colleagues know that you are focused right now

/focus [optional message]

Unfocus 👁

Stop your focus session

/unfocus

Sick 😷

Tell your colleagues that you got sick

/sick [reason]

Recovered 😷

Tell that you are back on track

/recovered [optional message]

FAQ

I cannot see the status indicator next to usernames

Make sure you have real names instead of usernames displayed in Slack.

Settings → Messages & Media → ✅ Display real names instead of usernames
